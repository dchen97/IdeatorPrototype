About the Program

What is the Ideator Program?

The Ideator Program is an approximately 3-month long competition that Dell holds every quarter for its IT Development Program members. It aims to encourage and support innovation within and outside of Dell by recognizing the ideators in our community.

Back to Top

When is the Ideator Program taking place?

The Ideator Program takes place every quarter. The upcoming Ideator session will start with Registration opening on August 29th, 2016.

Back to Top

What is the time commitment like to be an Ideator?

All of the Ideator Program activities should take teams no longer than a few hours each week to complete the tasks that they need. Suggested deadlines are listed on the Ideator Sharepoint calendar

Back to Top

How can I take part in the program?

If you're a part of the Dell ITDP program, then you are highly encouraged to register either as an individual or as a team for the program!

If you are not a part of the ITDP program, no worries! You still have a chance to contribute by submitting your ideas!

If you are an executive who would like to become a part of the selection committee, please feel free to contact the Ideator Program Steering Committee.
