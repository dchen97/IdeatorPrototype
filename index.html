Ideator Process

The Ideator program is a 3-month long event that will be held at Dell every quarter aimed towards encouraging and supporting innovation within Dell. Participants will have a unique opportunity to propose, prototype, and present their ideas to executives within Dell. Top ideas will go on to be funded and, if applicable, will be applied towards a patent.
